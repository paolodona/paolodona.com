A short history of the companies and roles I’ve been lucky to be part of.

  • Staycity Group

    Chief Information Officer · Jul 2019 – present

    Responsible for technology vision and strategy across the group, and for the full technology stack — IT, web and mobile apps, data warehouse and BI, security, AI, third-party development, and the IT team, vendors and system integrators.

  • Homestay.com

    Managing Director · Jun 2017 – Jun 2019
    CTO & Director · Jan 2012 – May 2017

    My third entrepreneurial venture. Co-founded the global homestay marketplace after my previous startup, NiftySchool, was acquired by Irish investors — the reason I moved to Ireland. Built the product, engineering, data, and multi-currency payments infrastructure from day one, and grew the team to ~30. Stepped up as Managing Director in 2017 to restructure the company and lead it from monthly losses to break-even through efficiency, cost, and conversion work.

  • NiftySchool.com

    Founder, Director & CTO · May 2009 – May 2012

    My second entrepreneurial venture. Founded and ran NiftySchool.com, a SaaS platform for language schools. Grew it from a London pilot to 100+ schools across three continents; acquired by Homestay Technologies in May 2012 and now part of the Homestay group.

  • Mobile Interactive Group

    Product Manager · Jun 2009 – Jun 2011
    Lead Developer · Jul 2008 – Jun 2009

    Joined as a senior Ruby on Rails developer on large-scale mobile platforms (content distribution, an O2 UK loyalty platform processing 500k+ top-ups/day), then moved into product to define and run an SMS content distribution platform and a multi-channel marketing platform used by O2, Vodafone, Three, Sky, Virgin Media and ITV — together delivering 10M+ messages/month.

  • SeeSaw srl

    Founder & Lead Developer · Sep 2006 – Sep 2008 · Verona, Italy

    My first entrepreneurial venture. Co-founded and ran a boutique software consultancy serving small and medium-sized customers across Italy, with projects ranging from websites and web apps to content management systems and back-end admin tools. Notable engagements included work for the Italian government (impresa.gov.it) and Q8 Petroleum. As part of SeeSaw I also ran the first commercial Ruby on Rails courses in Italy.
